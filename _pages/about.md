I am an Associate Professor of Management and Organizational Behavior at [EM Normandie Business School](https://en.em-normandie.com/) in France. I am also responsible for leading the graduate program in Digital Strategy and Innovation. Since January 2023, I have been serving as the Senior Editor of [Career Development International](https://www.emeraldgrouppublishing.com/journal/cdi), contributing to the advancement of career research.

During my doctoral research, I focused primarily on virtual teams and remote work, exploring the behavioral processes and organizational outcomes associated with digitalization. Simultaneously, I was keen on delving into the impact and darker aspects of digital media usage in broader social settings, ranging from education to dating. However, as my career progressed, I found myself drawn to more profound questions within the field of psychology and social sciences in general. My interests expanded to critical perspectives in the organizational psychology field, where I aim to address issues related to research inequalities, the dominance of the scientific elite, the sustainability of academic careers, and the challenges faced by academics.
